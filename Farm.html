The Core Experience

Farm City is built around an engaging and rewarding gameplay loop designed for long-term player retention. Players are constantly progressing, creating a powerful cycle of investment and achievement.

The Gameplay Loop: A Cycle of Growth

1. Start & Collect
Begin with a small plot and gather initial resources like coins and seeds.
2. Build & Plant
Construct the first farm and plant crops to begin production.
3. Harvest & Earn
Harvest the crops to earn valuable coins and experience points.
4. Expand & Customize
Use earnings to expand land, build new structures, and decorate.
5. Quest & Trade
Complete quests for big rewards and visit friends to trade resources.

12-Month Development Roadmap

Our development is structured into four distinct phases, ensuring a steady stream of new content and features to keep the community engaged from launch and beyond.

Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)

Launch with core building/farming mechanics, initial quests, and the Spring Festival event.

Phase 2: Content Expansion (Months 4-6)

Introduce animal barns, markets, a crafting system, and expand the world map with new biomes.

Phase 3: Social Features (Months 7-9)

Implement friend visits, trading, competitive leaderboards, and collaborative community guilds.

Phase 4: Seasonal Updates (Months 10-12)

Roll out major seasonal events (Summer, Autumn, Winter) with new content and gather player feedback.

Monetization Strategy

Our model is designed to be fair and player-friendly, blending direct purchases with value-added options to cater to a wide range of players.
